Intelligent Quarantine System

Safer alternative to immediate bans with rehabilitation options

Revolutionary Punishment System

Our quarantine system provides a safer, more humane alternative to immediate bans. Users who trigger protection measures are isolated for review rather than permanently removed, preventing false positive damage.

How Quarantine Works

  1. Isolation: User is moved to quarantine role with limited permissions
  2. Analysis: Automated threat assessment and evidence collection
  3. Notification: Moderators receive detailed incident reports
  4. Review: Manual or automated review process
  5. Resolution: Release, extend quarantine, or permanent action

Quarantine Features

  • Automatic Isolation: Instant containment without permanent damage
  • Evidence Collection: Detailed logs and threat analysis
  • Rehabilitation Options: Educational resources and warnings
  • Automated Release: Time-based or behavior-based release
  • Appeal System: User-initiated review requests
  • Escalation Paths: Progressive punishment system
