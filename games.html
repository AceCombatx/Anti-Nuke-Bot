Nuclear Simulator

Manage your own nuclear power plant

About the Game

Nuclear Simulator is a realistic power plant management game where players build and operate their own nuclear facility. Based on real-world physics and engineering principles, this simulation challenges players to balance safety, efficiency, and profitability while managing reactor operations, staff, and unexpected events.

Key Features

  • Realistic reactor physics and thermodynamics
  • Multiple reactor types (PWR, BWR, CANDU, etc.)
  • Staff management and training systems
  • Emergency scenarios and accident management
  • Economic simulation with power contracts and grid management
  • Research and development of new technologies
  • Regulatory compliance and inspections
  • Detailed performance metrics and statistics

Getting Started

To start playing Nuclear Simulator in your Discord server:

/nuclear start

This command initializes your nuclear power plant. From there, you can use various commands to manage your facility:

/nuclear status /nuclear build reactor [type] /nuclear operate power [percentage] /nuclear research [technology]

For a complete list of commands and detailed instructions, use /nuclear help in your Discord server.

Nuke Simulator

Develop your nation's nuclear weapons program

About the Game

Nuke Simulator is a geopolitical strategy game where players develop and manage their nation's nuclear weapons program. Based on historical technologies and international relations, this simulation challenges players to navigate the complex world of nuclear deterrence, diplomacy, and technological development.

Key Features

  • Historically accurate nuclear technologies and development paths
  • Research and development of fission, fusion, and delivery systems
  • Resource management (uranium, plutonium, tritium)
  • International diplomacy and sanctions system
  • Nuclear testing with realistic yields and detection
  • Facility development and security
  • Intelligence gathering on other nuclear powers
  • Global leaderboard and nation status tracking

Getting Started

To start playing Nuke Simulator in your Discord server:

/nuke start

This command establishes your nation's nuclear program. From there, you can use various commands to develop your arsenal:

/nuke status /nuke research /nuke develop /nuke test

For a complete list of commands and detailed instructions, use /nuke help in your Discord server.

Games FAQ

Common questions about our Discord games

Are these games free to play?

Yes, both Nuclear Simulator and Nuke Simulator are completely free to play. There are no premium features or pay-to-win mechanics. All game features are available to all players.

How do I start playing these games?

To start playing, first invite the bot to your Discord server using the "Invite Bot" button at the top of this page. Then, use the following commands to start each game:

  • For Nuclear Simulator: /nuclear start
  • For Nuke Simulator: /nuke start

After starting, you can use the help commands (/nuclear help or /nuke help) to learn more about the available commands and game mechanics.

Can I play both games in the same server?

Yes, you can play both Nuclear Simulator and Nuke Simulator in the same Discord server. The games operate independently and don't interfere with each other.

Are my game saves permanent?

Yes, your progress in both games is automatically saved to our database. You can continue your game at any time, even if the bot or your server restarts. Your progress is tied to your Discord user ID.

How realistic are these simulations?

Both games are designed to be educational and realistic while still being fun to play. They incorporate real-world physics, engineering principles, and historical accuracy, but some aspects are simplified for gameplay purposes.

Nuclear Simulator includes accurate reactor physics, cooling systems, and power generation mechanics based on real-world nuclear engineering.

Nuke Simulator features historically accurate nuclear weapon development paths, realistic material requirements, and authentic international relations mechanics.

Can I compete with other players?

Yes, both games include competitive elements:

  • Nuclear Simulator has leaderboards for power production, efficiency, and safety records.
  • Nuke Simulator features a global nuclear powers leaderboard where you can compare your nation's arsenal with others.

You can view these leaderboards using the /nuclear leaderboard and /nuke leaderboard commands.

How do I report bugs or suggest features?

You can report bugs or suggest features by joining our support server. Click the "Join Support Server" button in the Support section below to get help from our team and community.
